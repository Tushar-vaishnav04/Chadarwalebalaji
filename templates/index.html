Origin & History

श्री चादर वाले बालाजी मंदिर पाली, राजस्थान में स्थित एक पावन धाम है। इस मंदिर की स्थापना सैकड़ों वर्ष पूर्व हुई थी। ऐसी मान्यता है कि यहाँ स्थापित बालाजी महाराज की प्रतिमा स्वयंभू है और अत्यंत चमत्कारी है।

Sacred Significance

The temple is revered for its divine energy and the miraculous blessings bestowed upon devotees. The sacred chadar (holy cloth) draped upon the deity is a symbol of devotion, protection, and divine grace that draws pilgrims from across Rajasthan.

Why Devotees Visit

Thousands of devotees flock to this shrine seeking relief from ailments, resolution of life's challenges, and spiritual upliftment. Many testify to miraculous fulfillment of their wishes after sincere prayer and Chadar Seva.

Sacred Festivals

April

Hanuman Jayanti

The birth anniversary of Lord Hanuman, celebrated with great devotion, Sundar Kand recitation, and Chadar Seva.

April

Ram Navami

Lord Ram's birth celebration with special prayers, Ramayana recitation, and grand prasad distribution for devotees.

August

Balaji Mahotsav

The grand annual festival of the temple — three days of Maha Yagna, night-long bhajans, and a massive langar for all.
